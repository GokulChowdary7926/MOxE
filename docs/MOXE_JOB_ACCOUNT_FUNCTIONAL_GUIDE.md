## INTRODUCTION

The MOxE Job Account is a revolutionary career management platform that seamlessly integrates social networking with 24 powerful professional tools. It is designed for professionals, executives, and career-minded individuals who want to build their personal brand, manage their work, and advance their careers—all within a single, unified environment.

**IMPORTANT:** Job Accounts include **ALL features of the MOxE Personal Account** as a foundation. This means every social feature—posts, stories, reels, DMs, privacy controls, and more—is available alongside the 24 professional tools. This guide provides a complete, hierarchical breakdown of every feature available to a Job Account, from foundational social tools to advanced professional capabilities.

**Primary User:** Sarah (42, Chief Marketing Officer at a Fortune 500 company) – used throughout examples.

**Core Job Account Values:**
- Integrated professional-personal identity
- Executive-level networking
- Career development and recruitment
- Work-life balance and boundaries
- Authentic leadership presence

**Tier Options:**
- **No Free Tier:** Job Accounts are premium only
- **Paid Tier ($10/month):** Full access to all 24 tools, **Purple Verification Badge**, advanced analytics, priority support, **10GB cloud storage base**, additional storage available via paid plans

**Purple Verification Badge:** The purple checkmark (✓) appears next to the job account username, indicating:
- Identity has been verified
- Paid subscriber status
- Trust signal for recruiters and employers
- Priority in search results
- Enhanced impersonation protection

**Account Limits per Phone Number:** You can have up to **2 job accounts** plus **1 personal account** under the same phone number, for a total of 3 accounts.
